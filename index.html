I'm a second-year PhD student in Computer Science at EPFL, advised by Prof. Maria Brbić and Prof. Michael Moor. My research focuses on the post-training of multimodal language models and agents, as well as unsupervised learning. Previously, I was a master student at EPFL, where I worked with Prof. Sabine Süsstrunk. Prior to that, I obtained my bachelor degree at Wuhan University with Prof. Lei Yu.
